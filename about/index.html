About me

Ahoy there, I am Antonio Pavicevac-Ortiz!

I’d like to be the Leonard Lopate of Web Development and Design; What does that mean? Well, if you’re a listener to the James Beard award winning show on wnyc (93.9FM/820AM), you’d know how eclectic his shows topics are. Economics, Science, the Arts and current events are common place on his airwaves.

Going forward, my hope is to infuse my career with my interest in Graphic Design, Illustration and my insatiable interest in web development! Currently, I am a Front End Web Developer working at Avon Inc. in New York City!

My time is filled ‘Hello Worlding’ stuff. (e.g. HTML5, CSS, SASS, JS, AngularJS, Gulp, Ruby…)

My hope with this blog is to cronicle my progression in the dev world, specifically in regards to learning new stuff while actually living life. At the moment I am really focused on JavaScript.
